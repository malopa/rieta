What we Do Best

The main target customers of Rieta are small-scale farmers with average farm size of 5 acres and below as they occupy more than 70% of the cultivated area in Tanzania. Rieta packs seed in 2 kg and 1 kg packages to give farmers flexibility even those growing a quarter an acre. Rieta produces seed locally, processes and distributes within the country. The major areas targeted for maize and sorghum are highlands and medium altitude, drought prone areas and in short rain seasons especially in the central and lake zones.
