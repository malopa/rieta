About Rieta

Location

Rieta is headquartered in the Songwe region in the southern highlands of Tanzania. The company has started producing certified seed for distribution in the season 2020/2021. The company’s mission, among others, is to make farmers solve the existing challenges innovatively through the development, production, processing, and distribution of high-yielding and high quality seeds of various types suited to each agro-ecological condition in Tanzania.

Vision

Rieta’s vision is ‘to be a successful and most dependable partner in contributing to farmers’ development’. Rieta’s business is to develop, produce and distribute certified seeds of various crop varieties that are adapted to biotic and abiotic stresses at affordable prices and right packs. The initial target crops are maize, sorghum, sesame, groundnuts and sunflower. The varieties that are being produced by the company are UH6303 and Situka M1 for maize, Naliendele 2009 and Mnanje 2009 for groundnuts, Macia for sorghum, Record for sunflower, TXD 306 for rice and Lindi 02 for sesame. Rieta also provide extension service to small-scale farmers on good agronomic practices to improve farm productivity and to fully utilize farm resources potential.

Target

The main target customers of Rieta are small-scale farmers with average farm size of 5 acres and below as they occupy more than 70% of the cultivated area in Tanzania. Rieta packs seed in 2 kg and 1 kg packages to give farmers flexibility even those growing a quarter an acre. Rieta produces seed locally, processes and distributes within the country. The major areas targeted for maize and sorghum are highlands and medium altitude, drought prone areas and in short rain seasons especially in the central and lake zones.
